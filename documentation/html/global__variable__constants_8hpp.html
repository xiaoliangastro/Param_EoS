◆ EoS_type

enum EoS_type
Enumerator
TABULATED_ONLY 

interpolation only.

PIECEWISE_GAMMA 

piecewise polytropic expansion, conflict with causal.

PIECEWISE_PRESSURE 

piecewise polytropic expansion with pressure at different densities. (see Jiang20)

SPECTRAL_HBASE 

h-based spectral expansion and interpolation.

SPECTRAL_HBASE_CAUSAL 

note that Gamma will have different meaning, see Lindblom_18.

PWG_PHASE_TRANS 

piecewise gamma eos+phase transition(https://arxiv.org/pdf/1811.10929.pdf, Model2).

MITBAG_QUARK_STAR 

quark star model(Alford_2005_ApJ_629_969), this model will not have atmosphere implemented.

ADAPT_PIECEWISE 

adaptive rho border, with parameters: delta_rho*n+speed_of_sound*n.

CONS_CS 

constant speed of sound.

PIECE_SPEC_PHTR_CSS 

piecewise+spectral+phase transition+constant speed of sound (see Tang20)

PHYSICAL_SPEC_PHTR_CSS 

physical+spectral+phase transition+constant speed of sound (see Tang21)

PARAM_MU_CS 

parameterize the speed of sound, see https://doi.org/10.1038/s41567-020-0914-9
